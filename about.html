FREE COMPUTER LESSONS
JANUARY 2021

hidden

Free computer were introduced to give a chance to secondary school leaver who do not have access to a computer learn basic skills. Being an online institution, computers are one of the core instruments our students use during their studies.

Practicle and theoretical lessons are offered during the training. The thoery explains what the computer is and what they can use it for. The practical sessions gives hands-on experience manipulating documents, creating email account and composing their first email.

Please contact us for more information!

UNICAF University Logo

hidden

UNICAF is an abreviation for Univerity of NICosia in AFrica. It is an online and blended learning higher education platform in sub-Saharan Africa that is dedicated to making higher education of international standard accessible to African professionals and young school leavers.

The logo is made of the UNIC (University of Nicosia) color, Map of africa and AF (africa).
